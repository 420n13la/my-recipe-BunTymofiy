Ukrainian Recipe

Name: Borsch

Ingredients:
  • 1 lbs of beef
  • 1/3 medium head of cabbage
  • 4 potatoes
  • 1-2 onions
  • 1 carrot
  • 1-2 beets, boiled or canned
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • salt
  • dill
  • parsley
  • 1-2 laurel leaves
  • sour cream

Steps:
  1. Put beef is water.
  2. Boil the water.
  3. Cook the beef broth.
  4. When the meat is ready, remove it from the broth.
  5. Add sliced ​​potatoes and cabbage to the broth and cook them.
  6. While the cabbage and potatoes are cooking, prepare the basis for the borscht: chop carrot, onions and beets.
  7. Fry carrot and onions in butter.
  8. Add beet and tomato paste to carrot and onions, stir everything, and leave to simmer on the low-medium heat until ready.
  9. As soon as vegetables are ready, add them to the broth with cooked cabbage and potato, stir, add sliced meat, dill and parsley and heat it for 5 more minutes.
  10. Add laurel leaves and let it stand for 5-10 minutes before serving.
